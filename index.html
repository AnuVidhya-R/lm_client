About Chhota Bheem

Chhota Bheem is a popular animated series set in the village of Dholakpur. It tells the story of Bheem and his friends as they protect their home, spread joy, and teach values like courage, honesty, and teamwork.

Bheem

The brave and strong hero of Dholakpur, famous for his love of laddoos.

Chutki

Smart and kind, she is Bheem’s best friend and always helps the group.

Raju

Small but fearless, Raju never hesitates to fight for justice.
